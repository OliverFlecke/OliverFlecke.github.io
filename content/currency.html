A simple currency conversion tool, available on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch and Mac!

Features

  • Convert between multiple currencies at the same time
  • Support for 180+ different currencies, including popular crypto currencies
  • Offline support allows you to perform conversions even when you are without internet
  • Choose and sort which your currencies and sync them accross all your devices
  • Convert currencies directly from your Apple Watch
