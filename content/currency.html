A minimalistic currency conversion app, available on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch and Mac!

Features

  • Convert between multiple currencies at the same time
  • Support for 180+ different currencies, including popular crypto currencies
  • Offline support allows you to perform conversions even when you are without internet
  • Choose and sort which your currencies
  • Sync your settings and currencies accross your devices
  • Convert currencies directly from your Apple Watch

Privacy

This application does not access or store any kind of personal data. All settings and data are stored either on your device or on Apple's secure servers, and not accessible to us elsewhere.
