---
title: Convertible Currency app for iOS and more
date: 2021-11-01
---

Convertible currency is a simple app for all of Apple's devices, from iOS, WatchOS, iPad, and MacOS, which allows you to convert between many currencies.

It allows you to select and sort your favorite currencies, which is synced between all of your devices.
